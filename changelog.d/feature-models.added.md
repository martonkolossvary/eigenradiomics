Added a generalized per-feature outcome-modeling module. `compute_feature_associations(X, outcome, ...)` fits each feature against a continuous (OLS + HC3), survival (Cox), or binary (logistic) outcome across model tiers (e.g. univariable + clinically adjusted), with mixed/clustered variants when a `groups` identifier is given (MixedLM, random-intercept GLMM or cluster-robust GEE, and cluster-robust Cox), returning a `FeatureAssociationResult` (effect/CI/p, Benjamini-Hochberg FDR, fit diagnostics, catalog annotation, `top_hits`). `plot_volcano` renders the results with layouts chosen by panel count (1, 1x2, 1x3, 2x2, up to 3x3) and an outlier-aware x-axis, and the result bridges to the clustered heatmap (`.bar` / `.matrix`) and to a styled Excel workbook (`.to_excel`). Cox needs the `survival` extra (lifelines); binary/mixed need the `modeling` extra (statsmodels).
