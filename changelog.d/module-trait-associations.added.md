Added `compute_module_trait_associations(eigengenes, traits)` — the WGCNA-standard module-trait relationship. It correlates module eigengenes with clinical traits and returns `{"r", "p", "p_fdr"}` matrices (Benjamini-Hochberg across the table), giving the reduced feature space a principled downstream statistical summary. Accepts a `RadiomicsDataset` for the traits and encodes mixed-type traits via `encode_clinical_series`.
