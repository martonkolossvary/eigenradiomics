`RadiomicsPrepTransformer` and `RadiomicsFeatureRemover` no longer raise when fitted on a named DataFrame and then transformed with a bare NumPy array of the same width — a common `Pipeline`/`GridSearchCV` pattern (matching scikit-learn's warn-not-raise behaviour). `RadiomicsPrepTransformer` now also centres (near-)constant columns to zero under `standardize=True`, like `StandardScaler`, instead of passing them through at their raw magnitude.
