Added a structured `ReductionArtifacts` container and `BaseReducer.get_reduction_artifacts()`, giving every reducer one uniform way to expose its similarity matrix, linkage, per-feature cluster labels, feature order, and importances (all or a subset) for downstream plotting and analysis. `WGCNAReducer` populates all of these and gains two options: `correlation_method="spearman"` (rank-transformed network construction with leakage-safe original-scale eigengene loadings) and `power_min`/`power_max` to bound automatic soft-power selection.
