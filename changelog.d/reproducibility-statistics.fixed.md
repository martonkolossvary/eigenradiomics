Reproducibility statistics are now numerically honest at the edges: a perfect-agreement feature reports ICC 1.0 with an infinite F and p=0 (instead of a fabricated finite F from a 1e-15 floor), a fully-constant feature reports NaN throughout, the Fisher correlation CI returns NaN at |r|=1 (where the z-transform diverges) rather than a misleadingly narrow band, and the bootstrap ICC CI now requires at least half the resamples to be valid before reporting an interval.
