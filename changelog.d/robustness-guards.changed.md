Several silent-failure paths now fail fast or warn: `FeatureCatalog` raises on duplicate `config__feature_key` entries; `RadiomicsDataset.y()` raises when only one of `time`/`event` is set; `RadiomicsDataset.groups` raises on a group column with missing values; constructing a `RadiomicsDataset` with a catalog that matches none of the table's columns warns (then falls back to the name heuristic); and `WGCNAReducer.fit` warns about zero-variance features (left unassigned) and about producing no modules at all.
